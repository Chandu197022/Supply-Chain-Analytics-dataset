# Supply Chain & Inventory Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end Supply Chain & Inventory Analytics Dashboard developed using Power BI, SQL, Excel, and DAX. The dashboard helps analyze inventory performance, supplier efficiency, logistics operations, transportation costs, manufacturing quality, and overall supply chain effectiveness.

The objective of this project is to transform raw supply chain data into actionable business insights that support data-driven decision-making and operational optimization.

---

## Business Problem

Supply chain organizations face challenges related to inventory management, supplier performance, logistics efficiency, and product quality. This project addresses key business questions such as:

* Which products generate the highest revenue?
* Which products are overstocked or at risk of stockouts?
* Which suppliers perform best in terms of revenue and quality?
* How do shipping costs and delivery times impact operations?
* Which product categories experience the highest defect rates?
